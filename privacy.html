Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-28

Paris Street Bikes is a personal project. This policy explains how we handle data when generating and uploading cycling videos.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data from the public. The service is intended for the account owner’s use.

2. OAuth Tokens & Platform Data

When you connect a platform account (e.g., TikTok), the platform may provide access tokens and limited account information required to upload videos. We use this data only to perform the requested uploads.

3. Storage

If tokens are stored, they are stored securely for the sole purpose of keeping the connection active. You can revoke access at any time from your platform settings.

4. Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Content is uploaded only to the platform(s) you authorize.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect tokens and any operational logs. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Changes

We may update this policy by posting a new version on this page.
